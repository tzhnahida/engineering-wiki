# E-96 Resistors
# E-96 Resistors

## 1. 身份选型
Thick film chip resistor, E-96 series (1% tolerance). Available sizes 01005–2512.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — — size-dependent, typ. 25V (0201) to 200V (2512)
- **过载电压**: — — typically 2× rated voltage
- **工作温度**: −55°C to +155°C
- **额定功率**: — — size-dependent, typ. 1/32W (01005) to 1W (2512)

## 3. 推荐条件
- **降额使用**: Power ≤ 80% rated at ambient, further derate above 70°C
- **焊接**: Reflow per J-STD-020

## 4. 电气特性
- **电阻范围**: 10Ω–1MΩ (E-96 series)
- **公差**: ±1%
- **温度系数**: ±100ppm/°C to ±200ppm/°C (size-dependent)
- **最大工作电压**: Size-dependent (see section 2)

## 5. 机械特性
- **尺寸**: 01005 (0.4×0.2mm) to 2512 (6.4×3.2mm)
- **端子**: Ni barrier + Sn plating over Ag thick-film termination

## 6. 用途
General-purpose current limiting, voltage division, pull-up/pull-down, biasing.

## 7. 引脚/触点
2-terminal SMD — rectangular wrap-around end terminations.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: Chip sizes 01005–2512
- **安装**: SMT reflow soldering
- **卷装**: Tape & reel (see datasheet for quantity per size)
