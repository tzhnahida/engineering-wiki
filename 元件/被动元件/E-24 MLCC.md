# E-24 MLCC
# E-24 MLCC

## 1. 身份选型
Chip multilayer ceramic capacitor (MLCC), Class II (X5R/X7R), E-24 series. Sizes 01005–2220.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — — size and dielectric dependent, typ. 6.3V–100V
- **工作温度**: −55°C to +125°C (X7R) / −55°C to +85°C (X5R)
- **温度特性**: X5R (±15%), X7R (±15%)

## 3. 推荐条件
- **降额**: Run at ≤ 80% rated voltage for X7R, ≤ 50% for X5R (DC bias derating)
- **焊接**: Reflow per J-STD-020

## 4. 电气特性
- **电容范围**: — (typ. 0.1pF–100μF depending on size/voltage)
- **公差**: ±5%, ±10%, ±20% (E-24 preferred values)
- **损耗角正切 (DF)**: —
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 1000MΩ or ≥ 100MΩ·μF (whichever is smaller)

## 5. 机械特性
- **尺寸**: 01005 (0.4×0.2mm) to 2220 (5.7×5.0mm)
- **端子**: Ni/Sn plated

## 6. 用途
Decoupling, filtering, bulk storage, bypass, timing circuits.

## 7. 引脚/触点
2-terminal SMD.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: EIA sizes 01005–2220
- **安装**: SMT reflow soldering
- **卷装**: Tape & reel
