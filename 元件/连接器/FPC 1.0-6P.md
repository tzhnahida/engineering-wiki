# FPC 1.0-6P

## 1. 身份选型
FPC/FFC connector, 1.0mm pitch, 6-pin, SMT, bottom contact.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — V (typ. 50V)
- **额定电流**: 0.5A per pin (typical for 1.0mm pitch FPC)
- **工作温度**: −40°C to +85°C
- **插入寿命**: ≥ 20 cycles (typical)

## 3. 推荐条件
- **FPC 厚度**: 0.3±0.05mm
- **插入方向**: Horizontal (right-angle to board)
- **焊接**: Reflow soldering

## 4. 电气特性
- **接触电阻**: ≤ 30mΩ
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 100MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 200V AC / 1 min

## 5. 机械特性
- **间距**: 1.0mm
- **FPC 插入宽度**: — mm (6 × pitch + margins)
- **锁紧方式**: — (flip-lock / slide-lock / ZIF)

## 6. 用途
LCD display interconnect, camera module, touch panel, ribbon cable interface.

## 7. 引脚/触点
6 contacts — copper alloy, tin or gold plated. Lower contacts for FPC pads.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: SMT right-angle (horizontal mating)
- **安装**: Reflow soldering
- **卷装**: Tape & reel (embossed carrier)
