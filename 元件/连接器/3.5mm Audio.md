# 3.5mm Audio

## 1. 身份选型
PJ-320D 3.5mm TRS stereo audio jack, 5 contacts (stereo + detection), SMT.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — V (typ. 30V)
- **额定电流**: 0.5A per contact (typical)
- **工作温度**: −20°C to +70°C
- **插拔寿命**: ≥ 5,000 cycles

## 3. 推荐条件
- **插头规格**: 3.5mm TRS (tip/ring/sleeve)
- **焊接**: Wave or manual soldering (select SMT variant for reflow)

## 4. 电气特性
- **接触电阻**: ≤ 50mΩ
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 100MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 500V AC / 1 min
- **串扰**: — dB (audio frequency range)

## 5. 机械特性
- **尺寸**: — mm (L×W×H)
- **插孔内径**: 3.5mm
- **开关行程**: Detection switch activates on plug insertion

## 6. 用途
Stereo audio output/input, microphone input, headset detection.

## 7. 引脚/触点
5 contacts — Tip, Ring, Sleeve (TRS) + 2 independent detection switches. Contact material: phosphor bronze, selective gold plating.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: SMT right-angle (horizontal mating) or THT variant
- **安装**: Reflow soldering (SMT) or wave soldering (THT)
- **卷装**: Tape & reel (SMT)
