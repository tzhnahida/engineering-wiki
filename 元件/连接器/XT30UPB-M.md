# XT30UPB-M
# XT30UPB-M

## 1. 身份选型
XT30UPB male (plug) 2-pin power connector, Amass. Rated 30A continuous.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: 500V (per Amass XT30 spec)
- **额定电流**: 30A continuous (AWG 14 wire)
- **峰值电流**: 60A (< 5 seconds)
- **工作温度**: −20°C to +120°C
- **插拔寿命**: ≥ 1000 cycles

## 3. 推荐条件
- **匹配线规**: AWG 14–18 (silicone wire recommended)
- **配合**: XT30UPB female (XT30UPB30-F)
- **焊接**: Hand-solder (pre-tin wire, then solder into cup terminal)

## 4. 电气特性
- **接触电阻**: ≤ 0.5mΩ (mated pair)
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 100MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 1500V AC / 1 min

## 5. 机械特性
- **尺寸**: — mm (L×W×H)
- **端子间距**: 5.0mm (center-to-center)
- **外形**: Bullet-type contacts with keyed plastic housing (polyamide)

## 6. 用途
Battery/power connection — RC drones, FPV, power tools, portable power stations.

## 7. 引脚/触点
2 contacts — male bullet pins (gold-plated copper alloy). Housing: polyamide with positive locking latch.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: In-line cable plug (no PCB mount variant)
- **安装**: Hand-solder to wire; no PCB footprint
- **防呆**: Keyed shape — cannot reverse polarity
