# SMA RF
# SMA RF

## 1. 身份选型
SMA (SubMiniature version A) female RF coaxial connector, right-angle, 50Ω.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — V (typ. 335V peak — 50Ω system)
- **额定电流**: — A (center conductor)
- **工作温度**: −55°C to +155°C
- **插拔寿命**: ≥ 500 cycles
- **频率范围**: DC–18GHz (standard), DC–26.5GHz (high-performance)

## 3. 推荐条件
- **匹配线缆**: RG-174, RG-316, or semi-rigid coax
- **配合扭矩**: Hand-tight (0.45–0.9 N·m) — use torque wrench
- **焊接**: Center pin: soldering; outer shield: soldering or crimp

## 4. 电气特性
- **特性阻抗**: 50Ω
- **接触电阻 (中心)**: ≤ 6mΩ
- **接触电阻 (外导体)**: ≤ 1mΩ
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 5000MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 1000V AC (sea level)
- **VSWR**: ≤ 1.15 (DC–12.4GHz), ≤ 1.30 (12.4–18GHz)

## 5. 机械特性
- **尺寸**: — mm (hex coupling nut diameter 10.8mm)
- **接口螺纹**: 1/4"-36 UNS (coupling nut)
- **方向**: Right-angle

## 6. 用途
RF/antenna interface, GPS, Wi-Fi/BT, IoT wireless modules, SDR, test equipment.

## 7. 引脚/触点
2 conductors — center pin (signal) + outer shell (ground, shield). Female center receptacle (clamping). Outer body: hex brass nickel-plated.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: Panel-mount with flange / PCB edge-mount
- **安装**: Solder cup / PCB edge launch / bulkhead cable
- **配合**: SMA male (plug) with 1/4"-36 threaded coupling
