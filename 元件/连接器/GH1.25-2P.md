# GH1.25-2P

## 1. 身份选型
GH series wafer connector, 1.25mm pitch, 2-pin, wire-to-board, compatible with JST GH.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — V (typ. 50V)
- **额定电流**: 1A per pin (typical for 1.25mm pitch JST GH)
- **工作温度**: −25°C to +85°C
- **插入寿命**: ≥ 30 cycles

## 3. 推荐条件
- **匹配线规**: AWG 28–32
- **匹配外壳**: GH series crimp housing (JST part GH compatible)
- **焊接**: Wave or manual soldering

## 4. 电气特性
- **接触电阻**: ≤ 20mΩ
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 100MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 500V AC / 1 min

## 5. 机械特性
- **间距**: 1.25mm
- **尺寸**: — mm (L×W×H)
- **锁定方式**: Positive latch (ramp lock)

## 6. 用途
Internal wire-to-board interconnect — sensors, fans, small displays, battery leads.

## 7. 引脚/触点
2 pins — vertical or right-angle. Contact material: phosphor bronze, tin plated.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: Wafer (male pin header) — vertical or right-angle variants
- **安装**: Through-hole (DIP) — wave soldering
- **管装**: Tube or bag (not tape & reel)
