# DC-005H

## 1. 身份选型
DC-005H board-mount DC power barrel jack. Accepts 5.5×2.1mm and 5.5×2.5mm plugs.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — V (typ. 30V)
- **额定电流**: — A (typ. 2–5A for DC-005 series)
- **工作温度**: −20°C to +70°C
- **插拔寿命**: ≥ 5,000 cycles

## 3. 推荐条件
- **匹配插头**: 5.5×2.1mm (common) or 5.5×2.5mm center positive
- **焊接**: Wave soldering (THT pins + anchor leg)
- **内正外负**: Center pin = positive, outer shell = GND (typical wiring)

## 4. 电气特性
- **接触电阻**: ≤ 30mΩ (initial)
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 100MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 500V AC / 1 min

## 5. 机械特性
- **尺寸**: — mm (L×W×H)
- **外径**: 5.5mm
- **内径**: 2.1mm / 2.5mm compatible (spring contact adapts)
- **插孔深度**: — mm

## 6. 用途
Board-level DC power input — routers, development boards, audio equipment, consumer electronics.

## 7. 引脚/触点
3 terminals — center pin (+), outer sleeve (−), and normally-open detection switch (closes when plug inserted). Contact: copper alloy, tin plating.

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: Right-angle PCB jack, DIP (through-hole)
- **安装**: Wave soldering
- **锚定**: Extra support leg for mechanical retention
