# TF-PUSH

## 1. 身份选型
TF (microSD) card push-push connector, 8+2 pin with card detection, SMT.

## 2. 极限工况
- **额定电压**: — V (typ. 5V)
- **额定电流**: 0.5A per pin (typical)
- **工作温度**: −25°C to +85°C
- **插拔寿命**: ≥ 5,000 cycles
- **卡片插入力**: — N

## 3. 推荐条件
- **卡规格**: microSD / microSDHC / microSDXC compatible
- **焊接**: Reflow soldering — high-temp plastic required
- **ESD**: Add ESD protection on data lines

## 4. 电气特性
- **接触电阻**: ≤ 50mΩ
- **绝缘电阻**: ≥ 100MΩ @ 500V DC
- **耐压**: 500V AC / 1 min

- **尺寸**: — mm (L×W×H — push-push mechanism)

- **弹出行程**: — mm

## 6. 用途
External storage expansion, firmware update media, data logging.

## 7. 引脚/触点
10 pins total — 8 data/command (SDIO/SPI) + 2 card-detect (CD) switches. Contact: copper alloy, gold plated (selective).

## 8. 封装/安装
- **封装**: SMT right-angle, metal shell with locating pegs
- **安装**: Reflow soldering + through-hole anchor legs
- **卷装**: Tape & reel
